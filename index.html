About Us

Founded in Fall 2021, the Japanese Language & Culture Club at the University of Houston is a place for those interested in Japanese culture and the Japanese language to congregate and discuss thsoe topics. From academic assistance for those who are taking Japanese classes at UH, to general help & practice sessions, UH's JLCC has plenty of resources for those looking for help on their Japanese-learning jouney. We're an active community full of passionate Japanese enthusiasts eager to share our experiences with others.

We're open for anyone to join, so if you're interested in taking part in our activities & joining our community, feel free to join our discord server and see what we're all about!

Discord

The server is open to anyone studying at the University of Houston, so feel free to join if you're interested!
