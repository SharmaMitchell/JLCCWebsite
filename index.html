About Us

Founded in Fall 2021, the Japanese Language & Culture Club at the University of Houston is a place for those interested in Japanese culture and the Japanese language to congregate and discuss thsoe topics. From academic assistance for those who are taking Japanese classes at UH, to general help & practice sessions, UH's JLCC has plenty of resources for those looking for help on their Japanese-learning jouney. We're an active community full of passionate Japanese enthusiasts eager to share our experiences with others.

We're open for anyone to join, so if you're interested in taking part in our activities & joining our community, feel free to join our discord server and see what we're all about!

About Membership

Membership grants access to member-only community events! These include workshops, where JLCC teaches members how to make Japanese food.

Additionally, paying for membership helps fund JLCC events, and allows JLCC to increase community involvement. If you're a fan of what JLCC does, purchasing membership is a great way to support us & show your appreciation.

In order to receive memebrship & gain access to the above benefits, please pay dues via Zelle & fill out the membership form. Please be sure to write your full name and PSID in the Zelle message box!

Community

Discord

Join our Discord server to gain access to a community of over 100 other Japanese learners from the University of Houston. Have your burning Japanese questions answered by those who were once in your shoes. Participate in events hosted on Discord like Japanese Tables & impromptu gaming sessions.

The server is open to anyone studying at the University of Houston, so feel free to join if you're interested!
